2.1 Call Options

What is a call option? And how to do it.

Buying a Call Option:

Placing a bet that the stock price will go up in the future

Example:

You believe Disney stock will rise in the next month. Right now the stock is at $90. So you place a $25 bet that by June 15th Disney stock will pass $95.

Activity

Think of ONE stock you believe will go up in the next week, or month. And place a call option on it.
